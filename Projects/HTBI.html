Improving DEI Outcomes in the Hiring Process

Project

The lack of diversity in the workplace is a societal issue that demands effective solutions. However, promoting diversity in hiring comes with legal and ethical roadblocks which are in place in order to keep balance on equal opportunities. This challenge motivates my team to attempt to navigate this difficult process, and ultimately contribute to a more inclusive future in workplaces. This project aims to identify diverse candidates and match them with jobs based on their qualifications, while keeping explicit demographic information hidden from recruiters for legal purposes. In this project, my team and I faced the challenges of promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) goals in the workplace and in recruitment practices.

Overview

Problem

How might we improve the recruitment process by matching hiring needs to qualified, diverse candidates?

Outcome

My team and I created a web experience for recruiters that streamlines the process of identifying and recruiting diverse candidates in order to have a diverse group of candidates for a role relative to the company demographic.

Final application designs & solutions

Over the course of 4 months, I worked on a multimodal experience for museum experiences. This included building desktop, mobile, AR, VR applications and a design system that supports all it. This project aims to make museum experience more immersive & interactive.

Interactive Scavenger Hunts

◀ Desktop Experience

79% of survey respondents wished museums were more engaging. Users engage with museum artifacts through an interactive scavenger hunt experience. Online exhibitions can provide users with the option to dig deeper into the collection while also learning fun facts.

Robust filtering system

Desktop Experience ▶

Giving users a robust filtering system helps them find exact what they need. Some local museum goers already know the exhibits. They would rather find specific items they enjoy.

Personal collections & exhibits

◀ Desktop Experience

In order to preserve artifacts,museums maintain a barrier between the person and the artifact, leaving some museum goers feeling disconnected. This application allows users to create personal connection with artifacts by building collections for themselves or sharing them with others.

Mobile & AR Experience ▶

Artifact Extension

Allowing art to come alive and spill into the real world through this mobile AR experience. Users are immersed into the world of the artifact, leaving them with a deeper understanding of the piece.

AR Experience ▼

Artifact Extender

On a more educational note, I also created an AR experience that allows users to learn more about artifacts in a fun and interactive way. It allows museums to display content they may not otherwise be able.
